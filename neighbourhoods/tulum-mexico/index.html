TULUM

Luxury on the beach

Culture, nature, architecture and mobility have made Tulum one of the most spectacular places to live or invest in Mexico.

Tulum was a city created in 900 AD and was home to the royal family and nobility. An environment of Mayan culture that chose the place for the civilization to feel the proximity of the sea that would favour their movements. Besides the beaches, one of the great attractions of Tulum is its great cultural offer.
