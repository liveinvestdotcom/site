Paradise

Scuba diving

Rivera Maya is an undisputed paradise. Surrounded by jungle and crystal clear water that makes this city the world’s favourite destination. It is located in one of the most paradisiacal areas of the world, where the sea connects with the moon and the sun, which maintains the ancestral vibe of Tulum and the only one with direct access to the beach.

The paradise of Tulum offers for water sports lovers an infinity of activities to enjoy the incredible beaches and cenotes, which seem to be designed for sports such as diving in ancient cenotes, kite surfing, wake boarding, and paddle boarding
